About Birthday Reminder

Welcome to Birthday Reminder—a simple, heartfelt app to keep track of special days! Whether it’s a friend, family member, or even a company milestone, we’ll send you a warm nudge so you never miss a celebration.

Designed with care by AmirMahdi Tizchang, this is an open-source project. Feel free to explore the code, contribute, or send a pull request on GitHub.
